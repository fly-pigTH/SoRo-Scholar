**SoRo-Scholar** is a community-curated map of soft robotics research -- papers, researchers, labs, conferences, and tools. Whether you are a seasoned researcher, a new graduate student, or a curious engineer, this is your starting point for navigating the field of soft robotics.

Started by [Yinglei Zhu](https://fly-pigth.github.io/) and [Qiyao Wang](https://king-bridge.github.io/) in October 2025.

> **If you find this resource useful, please star this repo and share it with colleagues!**

## Papers

We organize the soft robotics literature into topical areas. Each category links to a dedicated page with year-by-year listings.

### Reviews & Surveys
> **[Full list &rarr;](papers/reviews.md)**

Comprehensive overviews of the field and its sub-domains.

- [Nature 2015] [Design, fabrication and control of soft robots](https://doi.org/10.1038/nature14543) -- D. Rus, M.T. Tolley
- [Angew. Chem. 2018] Soft Robotics -- G.M. Whitesides
- [Science Robotics 2016] Soft robotics: Technologies and systems pushing the boundaries of robot abilities -- C. Laschi, B. Mazzolai, M. Cianchetti
- [Soft Robotics 2014] Soft Robotics: A Perspective -- Current Trends and Prospects for the Future -- C. Majidi

### Actuation
> **[Full list &rarr;](papers/actuation.md)**

Pneumatic, hydraulic, electroactive, cable-driven, and shape-memory actuation technologies.

- [PNAS 2011] [Multigait soft robot](https://doi.org/10.1073/pnas.1116564108) -- R.F. Shepherd et al.
- [Nature 2016] [An integrated design and fabrication strategy for entirely soft, autonomous robots](https://doi.org/10.1038/nature19100) -- M. Wehner et al.
- [Science 2018] [Hydraulically amplified self-healing electrostatic actuators with muscle-like performance](https://doi.org/10.1126/science.aao6139) -- E. Acome et al.

### Sensing
> **[Full list &rarr;](papers/sensing.md)**

Stretchable, embedded, and distributed sensors for proprioception, touch, and environment awareness.

- [Science 2016] Highly stretchable electroluminescent skin for optical signaling and tactile sensing -- C. Larson et al.
- [Science Robotics 2016] Optoelectronically innervated soft prosthetic hand via stretchable optical waveguides -- H. Zhao et al.
- [Advanced Materials 2018] Soft Somatosensitive Actuators via Embedded 3D Printing -- R.L. Truby et al.

### Modeling & Control
> **[Full list &rarr;](papers/control.md)**

Model-based and learning-based approaches for soft robot control, including continuum mechanics and data-driven methods.

- [Soft Robotics 2018] Control Strategies for Soft Robotic Manipulators: A Survey -- T. George Thuruthel et al.
- [IEEE RAM 2016] Dynamic control of soft robots -- C. Della Santina et al.

### Fabrication
> **[Full list &rarr;](papers/fabrication.md)**

3D printing, molding, casting, and multi-material manufacturing for soft robots.

- [Nature Reviews Materials 2018] 3D printing of soft robotic systems -- T.J. Wallin, J. Pikul, R.F. Shepherd
- [Nature 2016] Printing soft matter in three dimensions -- R.L. Truby, J.A. Lewis

### Stiffness-Changing Mechanisms
> **[Full list &rarr;](papers/stiffness-changing.md)**

Granular jamming, phase-change materials, and other variable stiffness approaches for soft robots.

- [IEEE RAM 2016] Stiffening in Soft Robotics: A Review of the State of the Art -- M. Manti et al.

### Bio-Inspired Design
> **[Full list &rarr;](papers/bio-inspired.md)**

Designs inspired by octopuses, fish, caterpillars, plants, and other biological systems.

- [Nature 2021] Self-powered soft robot in the Mariana Trench -- G. Li et al.
- [Science Robotics 2018] Exploration of underwater life with an acoustically controlled soft robotic fish -- R.K. Katzschmann et al.
- [Bioinspiration & Biomimetics 2011] An octopus-bioinspired solution to movement and manipulation for soft robots -- M. Calisti et al.

### Medical & Healthcare
> **[Full list &rarr;](papers/medical.md)**

Soft robots for surgery, rehabilitation, prosthetics, and wearable assistive devices.

- [Nature Reviews Materials 2018] Biomedical applications of soft robotics -- M. Cianchetti et al.
- [Soft Robotics 2019] Soft Robotics in Minimally Invasive Surgery -- M. Runciman, A. Darzi, G.P. Mylonas
- [Robotics and Autonomous Systems 2015] Soft robotic glove for combined assistance and at-home rehabilitation -- P. Polygerinos et al.

### Human-Robot Interaction
> **[Full list &rarr;](papers/hri.md)**

Physical HRI, collaborative manipulation, and social soft robotics.

- [Science 2017] Soft wearable robots for physical assistance -- C.J. Walsh et al.
