# Matteo Cianchetti

**Affiliation:** Scuola Superiore Sant'Anna, The BioRobotics Institute, Pisa, Italy
**Lab:** Soft BioRobotics

## Research Interests
Biomedical soft robotics, variable stiffness mechanisms, surgical soft robots, and soft robot design for medical applications.

## Key Contributions
- Authored the influential "Biomedical applications of soft robotics" review in Nature Reviews Materials
- Advanced variable stiffness technologies for soft robotic surgery and manipulation
- Contributed to octopus-inspired soft robot arm development with Cecilia Laschi
- Led research on stiffness-changing mechanisms in soft robotics

## Selected Publications
- [Nature Reviews Materials 2018] Biomedical applications of soft robotics (M. Cianchetti, C. Laschi, A. Menciassi, P. Dario)
- [IEEE RAM 2016] Stiffening in Soft Robotics: A Review of the State of the Art (M. Manti, V. Cacucciolo, M. Cianchetti)
- [Science Robotics 2016] Soft robotics: Technologies and systems pushing the boundaries of robot abilities (C. Laschi, B. Mazzolai, M. Cianchetti)
