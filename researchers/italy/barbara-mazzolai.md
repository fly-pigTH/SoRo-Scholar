# Barbara Mazzolai

**Affiliation:** Istituto Italiano di Tecnologia (IIT), Genoa, Italy
**Lab:** Bioinspired Soft Robotics

## Research Interests
Plant-inspired robots, growing robots, bioinspired soft materials, environmental soft robotics, and nature-inspired design principles.

## Key Contributions
- Pioneered plant-inspired soft robots that can grow, adapt, and navigate through soil
- Co-authored the Science Robotics review on soft robotics technologies
- Developed bioinspired soft materials mimicking plant behavior
- Led the GrowBot project on plant-inspired growing robots

## Selected Publications
- [Science Robotics 2016] Soft robotics: Technologies and systems pushing the boundaries of robot abilities (C. Laschi, B. Mazzolai, M. Cianchetti)
- [Science 2016] Highly stretchable electroluminescent skin for optical signaling and tactile sensing (C. Larson et al.)
- Research on plant-inspired growing robots and adaptive soft materials
