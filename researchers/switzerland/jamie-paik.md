# Jamie Paik

**Affiliation:** EPFL (Ecole Polytechnique Federale de Lausanne), Switzerland
**Lab:** Reconfigurable Robotics Lab (RRL)

## Research Interests
Reconfigurable robotics, origami-inspired robots, soft actuators, shape-morphing structures, and modular soft robotic systems.

## Key Contributions
- Developed origami-inspired soft and reconfigurable robots
- Advanced shape-morphing robotic structures that can change form and function
- Pioneered modular soft robotic systems for versatile deployment
- Contributed to soft actuator design using novel materials and mechanisms

## Selected Publications
- Research on origami-inspired reconfigurable soft robots
- Work on shape-morphing soft actuators and structures
- Contributions to modular and deployable soft robotic systems
