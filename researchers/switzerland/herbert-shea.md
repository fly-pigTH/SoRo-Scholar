# Herbert Shea

**Affiliation:** EPFL (Ecole Polytechnique Federale de Lausanne), Switzerland
**Lab:** Soft Transducers Lab (LMTS)

## Research Interests
Dielectric elastomer actuators (DEAs), soft MEMS, micro-actuators, stretchable electronics, and electroactive polymer transducers.

## Key Contributions
- Developed high-performance dielectric elastomer actuators for insect-scale soft robots
- Demonstrated autonomous untethered soft robotic insects driven by DEAs
- Advanced micro-scale soft transducers and stretchable electronics
- Contributed to the understanding and optimization of electroactive polymer performance

## Selected Publications
- [Science Robotics 2019] An autonomous untethered fast soft robotic insect driven by low-voltage dielectric elastomer actuators (H. Shea et al.)
- Research on dielectric elastomer actuators and soft MEMS transducers
- Work on stretchable electronics and wearable soft devices
