# Koichi Suzumori

**Affiliation:** Tokyo Institute of Technology, Department of Mechanical Engineering
**Lab:** Suzumori-Endo Lab

## Research Interests
Pneumatic soft actuators, thin McKibben muscles, rubber artificial muscles, and soft robotic systems for human assistance.

## Key Contributions
- A pioneer in pneumatic soft actuators, with decades of foundational work
- Developed thin McKibben artificial muscles for compact soft robotic systems
- Advanced rubber-based artificial muscles for wearable and assistive applications
- Contributed to the long history of soft pneumatic actuation research in Japan

## Selected Publications
- Research on thin McKibben muscles and pneumatic artificial muscles
- Work on rubber actuators for soft robotic hands and assistive devices
- Contributions to soft pneumatic systems for human-robot interaction
