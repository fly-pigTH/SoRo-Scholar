# Pablo Valdivia y Alvarado

**Affiliation:** Singapore University of Technology and Design (SUTD)

## Research Interests
Bio-inspired aquatic robots, underwater soft robotics, fish-like locomotion, and compliant robotic systems for marine exploration.

## Key Contributions
- Developed bio-inspired soft robotic fish for underwater exploration
- Advanced compliant mechanisms for aquatic locomotion
- Contributed to the design of untethered soft underwater robots

## Selected Publications
- Research on bio-inspired aquatic soft robots and fish-like locomotion
- Work on compliant underwater robotic platforms
