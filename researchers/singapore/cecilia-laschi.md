# Cecilia Laschi

**Affiliation:** National University of Singapore (NUS); formerly Scuola Superiore Sant'Anna, Italy
**Lab:** Soft Robotics Lab

## Research Interests
Octopus-inspired robots, bioinspired soft robotics, soft manipulators, underwater soft robots, and the fundamental science of soft robotic systems.

## Key Contributions
- A founding figure of the soft robotics field, co-authored seminal reviews and research
- Led the development of octopus-inspired soft robot arms and manipulators
- Co-authored "Soft robotics: Technologies and systems pushing the boundaries of robot abilities" in Science Robotics
- Contributed to establishing soft robotics as a recognized discipline through journal editorships and community building

## Selected Publications

- [Advanced Robotics 2012] Soft Robot Arm Inspired by the Octopus (C. Laschi et al.)
