# Fumiya Iida

**Affiliation:** University of Cambridge, Department of Engineering
**Lab:** Bio-Inspired Robotics Lab

## Research Interests
Soft embodied intelligence, bio-inspired locomotion, morphological computation, evolutionary robotics, and the role of mechanical body properties in robotic behavior.

## Key Contributions
- Pioneered the concept of morphological computation in soft robots
- Developed bio-inspired locomotion systems that exploit body compliance
- Advanced evolutionary design methods for soft robotic structures
- Contributed to understanding how soft body dynamics can simplify robotic control

## Selected Publications
- Research on morphological computation and embodied intelligence
- Work on bio-inspired soft locomotion and legged robots
- Contributions to evolutionary soft robot design
