# Jonathan Rossiter

**Affiliation:** University of Bristol, Department of Engineering Mathematics
**Lab:** SoftLab

## Research Interests
Bio-inspired soft robotics, electroactive polymers, artificial muscles, soft energy harvesting, and biodegradable soft robots.

## Key Contributions
- Established SoftLab at Bristol, one of Europe's leading soft robotics groups
- Pioneered work on electroactive polymer actuators for soft robots
- Developed biodegradable and edible soft robots
- Advanced soft robotic systems for environmental monitoring and exploration

## Selected Publications
- Research on electroactive polymer-based soft actuators and artificial muscles
- Work on biodegradable soft robots and eco-friendly robotic materials
- Contributions to soft energy harvesting and self-powered soft systems
