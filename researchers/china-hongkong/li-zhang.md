# Li Zhang

**Affiliation:** The Chinese University of Hong Kong (CUHK), Department of Mechanical and Automation Engineering
**Lab:** Multi-Scale Medical Robotics Center

## Research Interests
Micro/nanorobotics, magnetic soft robots, small-scale medical robots, wireless actuation, and soft millirobots for biomedical applications.

## Key Contributions
- Pioneered magnetically actuated soft millirobots for biomedical applications
- Developed wireless control strategies for micro/nanoscale robots
- Advanced small-scale soft robots for targeted drug delivery and minimally invasive procedures
- Contributed to the design of shape-programmable magnetic soft materials

## Selected Publications
- Research on magnetically actuated soft millirobots and their biomedical applications
- Work on wireless micro/nanorobot control and navigation
- Contributions to shape-programmable magnetic soft composite materials
