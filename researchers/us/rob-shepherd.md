# Rob F. Shepherd

**Affiliation:** Cornell University, Sibley School of Mechanical and Aerospace Engineering
**Lab:** Organic Robotics Lab

## Research Interests
Stretchable optoelectronics, electroluminescent skins, optical sensors for soft robots, and multi-material manufacturing.

## Key Contributions
- Developed the multigait soft robot, one of the first untethered pneumatic soft robots
- Created highly stretchable electroluminescent skin for simultaneous signaling and sensing
- Advanced optoelectronic sensing methods for soft prosthetics
- Contributed to 3D printing techniques for soft robotic systems

## Selected Publications
- [PNAS 2011] Multigait soft robot (R.F. Shepherd et al.)
- [Science 2016] Highly stretchable electroluminescent skin for optical signaling and tactile sensing (C. Larson et al.)
- [Science Robotics 2016] Optoelectronically innervated soft prosthetic hand via stretchable optical waveguides (H. Zhao et al.)
- [Nature Reviews Materials 2018] 3D printing of soft robotic systems (T.J. Wallin, J. Pikul, R.F. Shepherd)
