# Rebecca Kramer-Bottiglio

**Affiliation:** Yale University, Department of Mechanical Engineering and Materials Science
**Lab:** Faboratory

## Research Interests
Adaptive morphology, variable stiffness materials, soft sensors, multi-functional soft materials, and reconfigurable soft robots that can change shape and properties on demand.

## Key Contributions
- Developed variable stiffness soft robotic skins and adaptive robot morphologies
- Pioneered multi-functional composite materials for soft robotics
- Advanced soft sensor integration for distributed sensing in soft robots
- Received the NSF CAREER Award for work on adaptive soft robotic systems

## Selected Publications
- Research on adaptive and reconfigurable soft robotic systems
- Work on variable stiffness composites using shape memory materials
- Contributions to soft sensor networks and e-textiles for robotics
