# Carmel Majidi

**Affiliation:** Carnegie Mellon University (CMU), Department of Mechanical Engineering
**Lab:** Soft Machines Lab

## Research Interests
Soft-matter electronics, liquid metal alloys for stretchable circuits, electronic skin (e-skin), and soft multifunctional materials.

## Key Contributions
- Published an early and influential perspective on soft robotics in the inaugural issue of Soft Robotics journal
- Pioneered the use of liquid metal (gallium-based alloys) for stretchable electronics and soft sensors
- Developed soft-matter electronic skin for robotic and prosthetic applications
- Advanced untethered soft robotic systems

## Selected Publications
- [Soft Robotics 2014] Soft Robotics: A Perspective -- Current Trends and Prospects for the Future (C. Majidi)
- [Nature Electronics 2018] Untethered soft robotics (S.I. Rich, R.J. Wood, C. Majidi)
