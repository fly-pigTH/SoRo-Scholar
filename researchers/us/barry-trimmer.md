# Barry Trimmer

**Affiliation:** Tufts University, Department of Biology
**Lab:** Trimmer Lab

## Research Interests
Biomechanics, caterpillar-inspired locomotion, soft-bodied organisms, and the biological foundations of soft robot design.

## Key Contributions
- A founding figure in bioinspired soft robotics, studying caterpillar locomotion as a model for soft robots
- Co-authored "Soft robotics: a bioinspired evolution in robotics" in Trends in Biotechnology
- Helped establish soft robotics as a recognized field through editorial leadership and community building
- Bridged biology and engineering to inform soft robot design principles

## Selected Publications
- [Trends in Biotechnology 2013] Soft robotics: a bioinspired evolution in robotics (S. Kim, C. Laschi, B. Trimmer)
- Research on caterpillar biomechanics and soft-bodied locomotion
