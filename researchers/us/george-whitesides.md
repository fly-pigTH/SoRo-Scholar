# George M. Whitesides

**Affiliation:** Harvard University, Department of Chemistry and Chemical Biology
**Lab:** Whitesides Research Group

## Research Interests
Soft robotics foundations, pneumatic soft actuators, soft lithography, materials chemistry, and microfluidics applied to soft robotic systems.

## Key Contributions
- A founding figure of the modern soft robotics field
- Developed the first pneumatic soft robots using elastomeric actuators (PneuNets)
- Published "Soft Robotics" in Angewandte Chemie, a defining perspective of the field
- His lab produced many foundational soft robotics papers (multigait robot, soft grippers, robotic tentacles)

## Selected Publications
- [Angew. Chem. 2018] Soft Robotics (G.M. Whitesides)
- [PNAS 2011] Multigait soft robot (R.F. Shepherd et al.)
- [Angew. Chem. 2011] Soft robotics for chemists (F. Ilievski et al.)
- [Advanced Materials 2013] Robotic tentacles with three-dimensional mobility based on flexible elastomers (R.V. Martinez et al.)
