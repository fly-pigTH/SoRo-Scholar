# Michael T. Tolley

**Affiliation:** University of California, San Diego (UCSD), Department of Mechanical and Aerospace Engineering
**Lab:** Bioinspired Robotics and Design Lab

## Research Interests
Soft robot design, origami-inspired robots, multi-material fabrication, and bioinspired locomotion.

## Key Contributions
- Co-authored the landmark Nature review "Design, fabrication and control of soft robots" with Daniela Rus
- Developed origami-inspired self-folding robots and structures
- Advanced multi-material 3D printing for soft-rigid hybrid robots
- Contributed to the design of walking and jumping soft robots

## Selected Publications
- [Nature 2015] Design, fabrication and control of soft robots (D. Rus, M.T. Tolley)
- [Advanced Materials 2014] 3D-printed, externally-and internally-reinforced silicone composites for soft robotics (N.W. Bartlett, M.T. Tolley et al.)
