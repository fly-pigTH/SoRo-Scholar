# Ryan L. Truby

**Affiliation:** Northwestern University, Department of Materials Science and Engineering
**Lab:** Truby Research Group

## Research Interests
3D printing of soft robots, somatosensitive actuators, embedded sensing, multi-material additive manufacturing, and bioinspired soft machines.

## Key Contributions
- Developed soft somatosensitive actuators with embedded 3D-printed sensors
- Co-authored "Printing soft matter in three dimensions" in Nature, a foundational review of soft matter 3D printing
- Co-developed the Octobot, the first entirely soft autonomous robot
- Pioneered architected soft actuators for artificial musculoskeletal systems
