# Huichan Zhao

**Affiliation:** Tsinghua University, Department of Mechanical Engineering
**Lab:** Soft & Hybrid Robotics Lab

## Research Interests
Soft sensors, optoelectronic sensing for soft robots, soft robot design, and embedded sensing in compliant systems.

## Key Contributions
- Co-developed optoelectronically innervated soft prosthetic hand using stretchable optical waveguides
- Advanced 3D printing techniques for soft robotic components
- Contributed to integrated sensing and actuation in soft robotic systems

## Selected Publications
- [Science Robotics 2016] Optoelectronically innervated soft prosthetic hand via stretchable optical waveguides (H. Zhao, K. O'Brien, S. Li, R.F. Shepherd)
- [Bioinspiration & Biomimetics 2015] 3D printing antagonistic systems of artificial muscle using projection stereolithography (B.N. Peele, T.J. Wallin, H. Zhao, R.F. Shepherd)
