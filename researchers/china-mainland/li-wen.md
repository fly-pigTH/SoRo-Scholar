# Li Wen

**Affiliation:** Beihang University, School of Mechanical Engineering and Automation

## Research Interests
Bio-inspired underwater soft robots, fish-like locomotion, flow sensing, and smart materials for aquatic robotic systems.

## Key Contributions
- Developed bio-inspired robotic fish using soft and compliant materials
- Advanced understanding of fish-like locomotion for soft underwater robots
- Contributed to flow sensing and hydrodynamic research for aquatic robotics
- Bridged biology and engineering in underwater soft robot design

## Selected Publications
- Research on bio-inspired soft robotic fish and underwater locomotion
- Work on flow sensing and hydrodynamics for aquatic soft robots
