# Guoying Gu

**Affiliation:** Shanghai Jiao Tong University (SJTU), School of Mechanical Engineering
**Lab:** Soft Robotics and Biodesign Lab

## Research Interests
Soft actuators, dielectric elastomer actuators, modeling and control of soft robots, and human-robot interaction with soft systems.

## Key Contributions
- Advanced dielectric elastomer actuator design and control for soft robotics
- Developed novel modeling approaches for soft actuators and compliant mechanisms
- Contributed to soft robotic gripper and manipulator design
- Active in bridging soft robotics with practical industrial applications

## Selected Publications
- Research on dielectric elastomer actuators for soft robotics
- Work on modeling and control of soft pneumatic actuators
- Contributions to soft robotic gripping and manipulation
