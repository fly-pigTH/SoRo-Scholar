# Review Papers & Surveys

A collection of review papers and surveys that provide comprehensive overviews of the soft robotics field and its sub-domains.


---

## Foundational Reviews

These landmark reviews define and survey the field of soft robotics as a whole.

[Nature 2015] [Design, fabrication and control of soft robots](https://doi.org/10.1038/nature14543) (Daniela Rus, Michael T. Tolley)

[Angew. Chem. Int. Ed. 2018] Soft Robotics (George M. Whitesides)

[Science Robotics 2016] Soft robotics: Technologies and systems pushing the boundaries of robot abilities (Cecilia Laschi, Barbara Mazzolai, Matteo Cianchetti)

[Trends in Biotechnology 2013] Soft robotics: a bioinspired evolution in robotics (Sungho Kim, Cecilia Laschi, Barry Trimmer)

[Soft Robotics 2014] Soft Robotics: A Perspective -- Current Trends and Prospects for the Future (Carmel Majidi)

[Applied Bionics and Biomechanics 2008] Soft robotics: Biological inspiration, state of the art, and future research (Deepak Trivedi, Christopher D. Rahn, William M. Kier, Ian D. Walker)

## Actuation & Materials Reviews

[Nature Electronics 2018] Untethered soft robotics (Sanlin I. Rich, Robert J. Wood, Carmel Majidi)

[Adv. Eng. Mater. 2017] Soft Robotics: Review of Fluid-Driven Intrinsically Soft Devices; Manufacturing, Sensing, Control, and Applications in Human-Robot Interaction (Panagiotis Polygerinos et al.)

## Application-Focused Reviews

[Nature Reviews Materials 2018] Biomedical applications of soft robotics (Matteo Cianchetti, Cecilia Laschi, Arianna Menciassi, Paolo Dario)

[Soft Robotics 2019] Soft Robotics in Minimally Invasive Surgery (Mark Runciman, Ara Darzi, George P. Mylonas)

[Nature Reviews Materials 2018] 3D printing of soft robotic systems (Thomas J. Wallin, Justin Pikul, Robert F. Shepherd)
